Makki di roti is a flat, unleavened Punjabi bread made from corn meal, primarily eaten in Punjab region of the Indian subcontinent.
Missi roti is hard to find and is made of whole wheat and grain flour dough, spiced with ground Punjabi masalas, eaten mainly with Curd.
